1. Purpose

This Agreement governs the disclosure of confidential information related to the Innovation Portal (the "Portal") in the context of the virtual meeting described above. The Portal has been developed by the Developer(s) (the "Developer"), who retains all intellectual property rights therein. OCHS is the commissioning institution facilitating this meeting and does not claim ownership of the Portal or its underlying technology.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed during or in connection with this meeting, including but not limited to: portal architecture, design specifications, feature roadmaps, user workflows, research data, technical documentation, source code, business strategies, and any discussions, presentations, or materials shared during the meeting.

3. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law, provided prompt written notice is given to OCHS.

4. Obligations

The undersigned agrees to: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) use it solely for evaluating and contributing to the Innovation Portal project; (c) not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent from the Developer(s); and (d) limit access to those with a legitimate need-to-know.

5. Term

This Agreement is effective from the date of signing and the obligations of confidentiality shall remain in force for a period of two (2) years thereafter.

6. Return or Destruction

Upon written request by the Developer(s) or OCHS, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or certifiably destroy all materials containing Confidential Information, including digital copies, and shall confirm such action in writing.

7. Intellectual Property Rights

All intellectual property rights, title, and interest in and to the Innovation Portal — including but not limited to its source code, architecture, design, documentation, algorithms, and any derivative works — are and shall remain the exclusive property of the Developer(s) who created them. This Agreement does not transfer, assign, or grant any ownership rights in the Innovation Portal to OCHS or any other party. OCHS acknowledges that its access to and use of the Innovation Portal is subject to separate written agreements with the Developer(s), and that no rights are granted by implication, estoppel, or otherwise through this NDA.

8. Remedies

Breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Developer(s). The Developer(s) shall be entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief — in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity — without the necessity of proving actual damages or posting a bond.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Sultanate of Oman. Any dispute shall be resolved in the competent courts of Oman.
